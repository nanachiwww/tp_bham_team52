Here are some recommendation to make you more healthier than before

1.Eat some Strawberries. They are highly nutritious and low in both carbs and calories. What more, They provide vitamin C, fiber, and manganese and make a delicious dessert.

2.Eat some Macadamia nuts. They are tasty and higher in monounsaturated fats and lower in omega-6 fatty acids than most other nuts.

3. Cucumbers make a refreshing snack. They are low in both carbs and calories, consisting mostly of water. They also contain small amounts of vitamin K and other nutrients.

4. Get some Shrimp. It tends to be low in fat and calories but high in protein. It also provides selenium and vitamin B12.

5. Eat less junk food In the long-term, eating junk food can lead to: type 2 diabetes. heart-related problems (such as cardiovascular disease, high blood pressure and cholesterol) overweight and obesity.
